About Us

Miso Moon Café is a dreamy fictional café inspired by Japanese comfort food, pastel dessert shops, and whimsical theme-park treats. The menu blends savory bowls, fluffy sweets, and colorful drinks with soft moon-and-star details for a cozy dining experience that feels both cute and comforting.
